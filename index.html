About Us

Meet Justin McFalls

As a second-generation plumber with over 20 years of hands-on experience, Justin McFalls founded Queen City Plumbing in 2009 with a simple mission: deliver honest, high-quality plumbing service that Charlotte families can rely on.

What started as a one-man operation has grown into one of Charlotte's most trusted plumbing companies, built entirely on word-of-mouth referrals and a commitment to doing the job right the first time. From routine maintenance to complex sewer line replacements, Justin and his team bring family-business values to every home they serve.
